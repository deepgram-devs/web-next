---
title: Real-Time Messaging Protocol (RTMP)
description: Protocol used to stream audio, video, and data over the internet. While RTMP was originally developed by Macromedia to stream between a Flash player and server, it continues to be used as an open-source protocol and is accepted by many streaming providers, such as Zoom.
